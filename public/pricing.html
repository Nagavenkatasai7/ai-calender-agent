Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 14-day trial work?
All new users get a 14-day trial with full access to Pro features. No credit card required. After the trial, you'll be moved to the Free plan unless you choose to upgrade.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal through our secure Stripe payment system.
How accurate is the AI parsing?
Our AI achieves 90%+ accuracy in parsing natural language into calendar events. We also have intelligent fallbacks to ensure your events are always created correctly.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment, no questions asked.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never store your calendar data permanently. We only access your Google Calendar with your explicit permission to create events.
